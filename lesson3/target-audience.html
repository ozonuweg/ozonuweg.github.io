Target Audience

My target audience is every one who resides in Preston,Soda Springs and Fish Haven. This includes people who are constantly on the move and will need to know what the weather is like in order to plan their movement. This is also for those residents who need to know the weather condition to plan their events and also people who just want to know what is going on around them.



Personas

KOPOKE

Kopoke is an assitant event planner who handles weddings, birthday parties, anniversaries and picnics. She plans both indoor and outdoor event and she needs to know the weather condition so she can know when to plan an outdoor event.



VWAIRE

Vwaire is a 21 year old college student who does not have a car and has to walk to campus and other places within her vicinity. She also needs to know about any social event thatis happening around her.



Scenarios

  1. Vwaire has a 11:30 AM class tomorrow and she wants to know what the weather will be like so she can plan her outfit.
  2. Kopoke needs to know what the weather will be like next weekend to see if her client should have an outdoor picnic or not
  3. Vwaire wants to hang out at the park and she needs to know if the weather is going to be favorable
  4. Should Kopoke have a pool party for her birthday in June?
  5. To save money, Vwaire sometimes rides her roommates bike to school. Will the current weather make her change her mode of transportation?

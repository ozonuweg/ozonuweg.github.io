Who We Are

This chamber is designed to help local business owners and professionals in Warri through networking, community, advertising, and promotion. Our target audience is every business owner who resides in Warri and have access to the internet. This particularly includes people who are just getting into business, people who have little or no knowledge on business, those who are very successful, even start-ups. This is also for people who just want to know what is going on around them.
